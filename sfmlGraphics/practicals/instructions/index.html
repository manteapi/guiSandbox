3D Computer Graphics Practicals

Introduction to Computer Graphics with OpenGL 4 and C++

About those practicals

Those practicals were designed for the course 3D Computer Graphics of the ENSIMAG Computer Science school. The goal of such a course is to introduce Computer Graphics: Modeling, Animation and Rendering. In those practicals, we will use modern OpenGL and C++. Students are new to both OpenGL and C++. As such, we will do simple things and those practicals can be easily followed by anyone with some basis in Computer Science (as long as the references given in the tutorials are read). At the end of practicals, students will realize a graded project. Here is the overview of the practicals:

  • Pratical 1 - A quick & dirty introduction to OpenGL
  • Pratical 2 - 3D primitive modeling
  • Pratical 3 - Hierarchical modeling
  • Pratical 4 - Procedural animation
  • Pratical 5 - Physics-based animation
  • Pratical 6 - Local illumination
  • Pratical 7 - Texturing

This format for the practical was initiated in January 2016. Thus, you may notice some imperfections and lack of polish. You can help us to improve the practicals by notifying us any problem or issue. Now, let's get started with the first practical!
